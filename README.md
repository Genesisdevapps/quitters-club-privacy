# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Quitters Club ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Quitters Club mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

Quitters Club does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal identification information.

The App processes data voluntarily entered by the user, including but not limited to:
- Quit date and time
- Smoking habits (e.g., cigarettes per day, pack price)
- Craving and trigger logs
- Progress and usage statistics

## Data Usage

The data processed by the App is used solely to provide core functionality, display progress insights, and support the user experience.  
We do not sell, rent, or share user data for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Data Storage

Data entered into the App is primarily processed on the user's device.  
Quitters Club does not use user data for profiling, advertising, or commercial resale.

## Third-Party Services

Quitters Club does not use third-party services for advertising or user profiling.  
Limited third-party services may be used strictly for app distribution, performance monitoring, or stability purposes, in accordance with applicable privacy standards.
